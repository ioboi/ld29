Instructions

Place mines on the surface. Each mine costs 10$.
Change the drill-direction by clicking on the drill.
You can not drill through stone or another driller!!! (The game does not end)
Pause the game at anytime by hitting spacebar.
UP/DOWN = W/S
Smaragds give 2 or 4$.
Diamonds give 5 or 10$.
The game ends if you are hitting the bedrock or all driller are stuck and you don't have enough money
